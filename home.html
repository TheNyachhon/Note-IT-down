How to Use?

  • Create a new note using the "+ New Note" button on the top right corner.
  • To start writing, click the edit button on top of the note card
  • Do the needful, and don't forget to hit the save icon! Otherwise the note will not be saved.
  • To delete a note, simply hit the cross button on top of the note card you wish to delete.

    • Thats it! Its as simple as that
    All your notes are saved locally in your device and no signing in is required!

    Disclaimer: if you clean your browser cookies, the notes will also be deleted.
